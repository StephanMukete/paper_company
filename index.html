About ActivSpaces

ActivSpaces exists to help young techies and technology entrepreneurs in Cameroon bring their ground breaking ideas to light. ActivSpaces was created in 2010 and is registered as a nonprofit Association in Cameroon. ActivSpaces currently operates two hub locations in Buea and Douala respectively.
